Maldives

The Maldives are a tropical paradise with crystal-clear waters and coral reefs. What is Maldives famous for? The Maldives is famous for a number of reasons, including its vibrant culture, pristine beaches, sprawling greenery, and fascinating landmarks. Apart from the numerous enchanting landmarks, Maldives is also famous for water sports and other adventure activities. How much is Maldives trip cost? On average, a Maldives trip cost for couple in US dollars should fall between $3,000 and $6,000 for a week. This includes airfare, accommodation, meals, and transportation. This estimation provides a balance of comfort and experience, ensuring you enjoy the Maldives to its fullest.
